## Core Technology: The ZimmerOasis Living System

ZimmerOasis's unique selling point is the ability to **cultivate real, living moss permanently on furniture surfaces**. Unlike preserved (dead) moss products, our moss remains biologically active – it purifies the air, regulates humidity, and evolves visually over time. The technological solution consists of a patentable multi-layer system.

### Multi-Layer Construction: From Furniture Surface to Moss

Each ZimmerOasis piece of furniture consists of **five functional layers** that keep the moss alive permanently:

| Layer | Material | Function |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **1. Furniture Body** | Solid wood / plywood, water-repellent coated | Structural support, moisture protection |
| **2. Water Barrier** | EPDM membrane (1.5 mm) | Prevents moisture transfer into the furniture body |
| **3. Capillary Irrigation Layer** | Capillary mats made from recycled PET fleece | Even water distribution through capillary action |
| **4. Substrate Layer** | Sphagnum peat moss, perlite, coconut fiber (ratio 60:20:20) | Water storage, nutrient carrier, moss root anchoring |
| **5. Living Moss Layer** | Selected moss species (see below) | Biologically active surface, aesthetic value |

### Capillary Irrigation System: Water Without Electricity

The core of the technology is a **passive capillary irrigation system** that operates without electric pumps:

1. **Integrated Water Tank** (1-3 liters, depending on furniture size) is located in the base or back panel of the furniture piece
2. **Capillary Mats** made from recycled PET fleece transport water upward into the substrate layer through natural capillary action
3. **Wick System**: Textile wicks connect the water tank to the capillary layer, ensuring even moisture distribution
4. **Overflow Protection**: A float valve in the tank prevents overfilling

**Advantages of the Passive System**:
- No electricity required for irrigation
- Silent and low-maintenance
- Even moisture distribution across the entire surface
- Tank range: **4-8 weeks** depending on room climate and season

### Nutrient Supply: Biological Balance

Compared to higher plants, mosses have **extremely low nutrient requirements** because they have no true roots and absorb nutrients directly through their leaf surfaces:

- **Nitrogen (N)**: Absorbed from ambient air and dissolved rainwater
- **Minerals (K, Mg, Ca)**: Minimal amounts provided by the sphagnum substrate
- **pH Regulation**: The sphagnum substrate maintains a slightly acidic pH of 4.5-6.0 – optimal for mosses
- **Nutrient Solution**: A diluted moss fertilizer (N-P-K 2-1-1, 1:1000) is added to the water tank every six months

**No fertilizing required by the user** – the Smart Care app simply reminds them every 6 months about the refill process.

### Sensor Technology: Intelligent Irrigation Monitoring

Each piece of furniture features an **embedded sensor module** (ESP32-based, ultra-compact):

| Sensor | Measurement | Function |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Capacitive Moisture Sensor** | Substrate moisture (%) | Monitors water content in the substrate |
| **Level Sensor** | Tank fill level (ml) | Measures remaining water supply |
| **Temperature Sensor** (NTC) | Substrate temperature (°C) | Detects frost or heat danger |
| **Light Sensor** (LDR) | Ambient light (Lux) | Monitors lighting conditions |

**Smart Care App** (iOS & Android):
- **Push Notification** when the water tank needs refilling
- **Status Overview**: Moisture, temperature, light at a glance
- **Historical Data**: Trend charts for optimal care
- **Tips**: Automatic recommendations for unfavorable conditions (e.g., too much direct sunlight)
- **Smart Home Integration**: Compatible with Apple HomeKit, Google Home, Amazon Alexa

### Moss Species Selection: Robust and Aesthetic

For use on furniture surfaces, **three moss species** were selected for their robustness, low light requirements, and appealing aesthetics:

| Moss Species | Properties | Application |
|-------------|------------|-------------|
| **Leucobryum glaucum** (Cushion Moss) | Cushion-shaped, silver-green, extremely drought-resistant | Seating surfaces, armrests, curved surfaces |
| **Hypnum cupressiforme** (Cypress-leaved Plait Moss) | Flat-growing, deep green, rapid regeneration | Table tops, flat panels, Modular inserts |
| **Thuidium tamariscinum** (Tamarisk Moss) | Fern-like, decorative, shade-tolerant | Decorative elements, picture frames |

**All three species**:
- Require **no direct sunlight** (ideal for indoor spaces, 300-800 Lux sufficient)
- Survive **brief drying periods** and regenerate after watering
- Grow slowly (1-3 mm/month) – the surface does not need trimming
- Are **non-allergenic** and non-toxic for pets and children

### Lifespan and Maintenance: Smart Care in Daily Life

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Expected Lifespan** | 3-5 years without moss replacement with proper care |
| **Water Tank Refill** | Every 4-8 weeks (app notification) |
| **Nutrient Solution** | Add to tank every 6 months (included portion packets) |
| **Moss Replacement** | Modular moss panels can be easily replaced by the user |
| **Cleaning** | Carefully remove dust with compressed air or soft brush |

**The Decisive Advantage**: Thanks to the Smart Care system, maintenance effort is reduced to **less than 5 minutes per month** – comparable to watering a single houseplant.
