# ZimmerOasis - Visual Style Guide

This guide defines the aesthetic for all "ZimmerOasis" visual assets.

## Core Aesthetic
- **Philosophy**: "Nature indoors, elevated."
- **Keywords**: Clean, Minimalist, Scandinavian, Organic, Breathable.
- **Lighting**: Bright, natural daylight (Golden hour or soft morning light). Large windows, airy atmosphere.

## Design Elements
- **Materials**: 
    - Light woods (Oak, Birch, Ash).
    - Taktile fabrics (Linen, Wool, soft Velvet in neutral tones).
    - **Moss**: Vibrant, lush, deep green, diverse textures (cushion moss, sheet moss). Must look perfectly integrated into furniture.
- **Architecture**: Modern, minimalist interiors. High ceilings, white or light gray walls.

## Photography Specs
- **Style**: Professional product photography or high-end architectural photography.
- **Backgrounds**: Clean, non-distracting. White backgrounds for product shots, modern minimalist rooms for lifestyle shots.
- **Quality**: Photorealistic, 8K, high detail.

## Prompt Suffix
`Scandinavian minimalist design, natural lighting, photorealistic, professional product photography, 8k --v 6`
