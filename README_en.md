# Project: ZimmerOasis - Living Moss Furniture

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Important Note:** This project is about making the "Living Furniture" concept tangible. There is no company behind it; this site exists solely to make this vision accessible.

## 1. Project Overview

**Mission:** To seamlessly integrate the benefits of living nature into urban homes through a line of modular, tech-infused furniture. ZimmerOasis designs and sells furniture pieces whose surfaces are covered with living, air-purifying moss.

**Core Concept:** The project addresses the "nature deficit" in modern city living by transforming everyday furniture into beautiful, low-maintenance, and functional ecosystems. This is achieved through "Biophilic Design," connecting humans with nature to improve well-being.

**Brand Name:** ZimmerOasis
**Brand Identity:** "Return to Nature." The brand should feel innovative, organic, calming, and intelligent. It bridges the gap between technology and nature, and between home decor and wellness.

## 2. Product Concept & Technology

### 2.1. Core Product Idea
The furniture is not entirely made of moss. It follows a **hybrid design principle**:
* **Seating/High-Contact Surfaces:** Made from traditional, high-quality, comfortable fabrics (linen, wool, velvet).
* **Structural/Aesthetic Surfaces:** (e.g., armrests, back panels, sides) are fitted with our proprietary **Living Moss Panels**.

### 2.2. The Moss Panel System
This is the core innovation.
* **Modular & Exchangeable:** The moss grows on a self-contained, lightweight substrate panel. This panel can be easily attached to or detached from the furniture frame.
* **Benefits:**
    * **Maintenance:** Damaged or aging panels can be swapped out by the customer.
    * **Customization:** Customers could potentially switch between different types of moss panels (e.g., different shades or textures) in the future.
* **Watering:** Each panel is fed by an integrated, hidden water reservoir via a capillary wicking system. This ensures the moss gets the exact amount of water it needs without user intervention.

### 2.3. Tiered Smart Integration
The technology is scaled across three price segments to maximize market reach.

* **Tier 1: Economy (e.g., FLÖDA Collection)**
* **No Smart Features.**
* Includes a simple, non-electric **physical water level indicator** (e.g., a small floating red stick) on the reservoir. Purely analog and cost-effective.

* **Tier 2: Mid-Range (e.g., KÄLLA Collection)**
* **Basic Smart Functionality.**
* Equipped with a sensor in the water reservoir.
* Connects to the **ZimmerOasis mobile app** via Bluetooth/Wi-Fi.
* The app sends a single, crucial notification: **"Time to refill the water reservoir."**

* **Tier 3: Premium (e.g., BIOS Collection)**
* **Full Smart Home Integration.**
* Includes advanced sensors within the moss panel itself.
* The app tracks and displays:
* Water level
* Ambient room humidity
* Room temperature
* Air quality metrics (VOCs, particulates)
* Features **Smart Home compatibility** (Apple HomeKit, Google Home, Amazon Alexa) for routines and data monitoring.

## 3. Business Case & Strategy

### 3.1. Value Proposition
* **Aesthetic:** Unique, living statement furniture that serves as a natural centerpiece.
* **Health & Wellness:** Proven air purification (filters dust/VOCs) and passive humidity regulation. Stress reduction through biophilia.
* **Low Maintenance:** The "set it and forget it" watering system removes the biggest hurdle for owning plants.
* **Modularity:** The modular design and exchangeable panels offer longevity and customization.

### 3.2. Monetization Streams
1.  **Complete Furniture Sales:** Selling the fully designed ZimmerOasis furniture pieces across the three collections (Economy, Mid-Range, Premium).
2.  **Recurring Revenue - Replacement Panels:** Selling new moss panels to existing customers for replacement or aesthetic updates.
3.  **Market-Entry Product - "Modular Furniture Upgrades":** This is a key strategic pillar.
    * **Concept:** Sell standalone Living Moss Panels designed to perfectly fit common modular furniture (e.g., side panels for [shelf systems](https://www.ikea.com/de/de/cat/kallax-serie-27534/), back panels for [cabinet series](https://www.ikea.com/de/de/cat/besta-serie-11794/), armrest covers for [sofa systems](https://www.ikea.com/de/de/cat/sofas-serie-10661/)).
    * **Advantage:** Taps into a massive existing market. Low-risk purchase for customers to try the technology. Acts as a gateway to the full ZimmerOasis ecosystem.

### 3.3. Competitor Analysis
* **Direct Competitors:** Very few. Consists mainly of small, boutique design studios or artists creating one-off, non-scalable pieces. *Our advantage: Scalability, modularity, and smart tech integration.*
* **Indirect Competitors:**
    * **Living Wall Companies (e.g., Plantui, G-Sky):** Compete on the "green interior" concept. *Our advantage: Dual functionality (furniture + plant) and typically a lower price point and easier installation.*
    * **High-End Plant Retailers & Plantscaping Services:** Compete on bringing nature indoors. *Our advantage: Integrated, automated care and a unique form factor.*
    * **Traditional & Designer Furniture Brands (e.g., Westwing, Vitra):** Compete on the furniture itself. *Our advantage: The unique, patented living/tech feature is our USP.*

## 5. Website & Webshop - Functional Requirements

* **Structure:**
    * **Homepage:** Hero section with the core value proposition.
    * **Collections/Shop:** Main e-commerce section, filterable by collection (FLÖDA, KÄLLA, BIOS) and product type.

    * **FAQ:** Answering key questions about care, smell, insects, vacation, etc.

    * Product pages must allow for selection of variants: fabric color, wood type, and collection/tech tier.
